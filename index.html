A warm welcome to my site

A lot has been happening for me musically this year and I hope that all that you see in this website will be an overview of this. All the visual and audio content in this website has taken place in late 2012-2013.


Latest News:

There will be a new collection of Country and Irish songs from me around about 1st March 2013. The songs have been carefully chosen and recorded in No Sweat Studios, Coleraine.

The album has been recorded, mixed and engineered by my friend for many years, Clive Culbertson who has made quite an impression on the musical scene here and abroad over the years.

With the onset of a more clement climate, there will be a DVD to accompany the album which should be available in the early summer of 2013. The video footage will be filmed and produced by Ian Linton of Linton Film Productions, Garvagh and Ivan Scott of Medusa Photography.

I’m sure everyone who is a fan of Country & Irish Music is aware of all the shows. on Sky 191 and Free Sat 403 shown most afternoons and nights of the week.
